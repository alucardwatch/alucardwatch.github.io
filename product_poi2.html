Aegis of Flores History

Like many other islands in Indonesia, Flores can be considered as a unique island in Indonesia. Taken from Portuguese language, the Flores itself means flower and the name itself match the geographic condition of Flores Island. Surrounded by ocean and beautiful scenery, Flores island is the hidden paradise after Bali island.

Located in Central Indonesia between Sumba and Timor Islands and as one of the island in Indonesia, Flores is certainly have many traditional tribes and unique culture. Alucard want to create something that will represent the uniqueness and also pride of Indonesia therefore as an expertise in classic watch, Alucard want to transform the beauty of Indonesia into a classic watch accessory that you can wear with pride and fashion.

Inspired from the beauty of Flores, Alucard has created Aegis of Flores to represent the beauty of Flores, made with special Teak Wood as the main material, with unique brown color, Aegis of Flores is suitable to represent the unique aura of Flores island and accompany you as time reminder.

Teak wood is known for its elegance and durability, it also has its own scent, making it a great combination of unique and natural fashion accessory for you.

By wearing Aegis of Flores, Alucard hopes that you will be as unique and beautiful like what Flores island represent. You have to show that your uniqueness can bring something positive to you and the people around you. You can accept any kind of friends from different culture, religion, and ethics just like when you are living in Indonesia with many rich heritages. For Indonesia people, Aegis of Flores is the way for them to remember the spirit of Nusantara.

Alucard want to give not only watch but a special classic watch with a sense of pride to the wearer. Alucard want to creates watches that offer aesthetic sense, deep meaning, flexibility for everyone, and comfortableness. By wearing Aegis of Flores, you can feel the spirit of Indonesia, from Teak wood and its rich brown color as your watch accessory, you will be a new person. You will have the uniqueness and strength to achieve your everyday goals.
